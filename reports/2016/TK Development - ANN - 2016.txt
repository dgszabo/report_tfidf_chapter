STATUTORY ANNUAL CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBIL ITY STATEMENT; SE E SECTION 99AOF THE DANISH F INANCIAL STATEMENTS ACTIn addition to carrying on profitable business activities, TK Developmentintends to adhere to and expand the Group’s ethical,social and environmental responsibilities as a business corporation.TK Development fundamentally endorses the UN’s ten socialresponsibility principles, but has not acceded to the UN GlobalCompact.In light of the Company’s size and activities and the Group’soperating markets, the Board of Directors has decided not toadopt policies for the voluntary integration of corporate socialresponsibility or human rights and climate policies. The Boardof Directors will regularly assess the need for policies in thisarea.
